# TGB Enterprise – Design Guidelines & Freeze Protocol

**STATUS: DESIGN FREEZE ACTIVE**

The website has reached production quality. The visual system is considered stable, and the priority has shifted from design experimentation to business outcomes.

## Design Freeze Rules
Do NOT undertake any further visual redesigns unless explicitly directed due to one of the following reasons:
1. Real users struggle to understand the content.
2. Analytics indicate conversion issues.
3. Clients provide consistent qualitative feedback.
4. Performance issues emerge.

**Strictly Avoid Introducing:**
* New design trends or micro-aesthetics
* Additional animations or interaction bouncy-ness
* WebGL experiences
* Custom cursors
* Magnetic interactions
* Layout redesigns

## Future Priorities
All future improvements and agent tasks should focus on:
* Content quality
* Photography
* Project case studies
* Copywriting
* Performance optimization
* Accessibility
* SEO
